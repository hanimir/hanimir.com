Frequently asked questions

Answers to common questions about working together, scheduling, and what to expect.

Do you offer telehealth?

I offer secure video appointments to adults across California. In-person sessions are available in the San Francisco Bay Area.

How do I get started?

The first step is a brief consultation to understand your goals and determine fit. You can request a consultation on the contact page.

What is your approach to medication?

Medication is used thoughtfully and only when helpful or indicated. We will review options together and revisit the plan as your needs evolve.

Do you work with other therapists?

Yes. I often collaborate with therapists and other clinicians to provide coordinated care and support integration work.

Are you in-network with insurance?

I am an out-of-network provider and can provide superbills for reimbursement. Coverage varies by plan.

What issues do you specialize in?

I work with adults seeking support for anxiety, OCD, ADHD, trauma, relational challenges, life transitions, and identity exploration.
