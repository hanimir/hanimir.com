Services

I offer personalized psychiatric care for adults, with a focus on psychotherapy, careful medication management, and collaborative treatment planning. Appointments are available via secure telehealth for residents across California, with in-person sessions offered in the San Francisco Bay Area.

Psychotherapy

Individual therapy that integrates psychodynamic, relational, and mindfulness-based approaches. We focus on patterns, attachment, emotional regulation, and strengthening your internal resources.

Medication management

Evidence-based psychiatric care with a conservative prescribing philosophy. Medication is considered when it complements psychotherapy and aligns with your values.

Combined treatment

For many patients, psychotherapy and medication together can provide greater stability, insight, and resilience. We revisit your plan regularly.

Psychedelic integration

Support for individuals engaging in ketamine-assisted psychotherapy or other legally sanctioned psychedelic therapies, with focus on preparation and integration.

Clinical focus areas

  • Anxiety disorders, OCD, and panic
  • ADHD and executive function challenges
  • Trauma, attachment, and relational patterns
  • Depression, grief, and life transitions
  • Identity, purpose, and meaning-making
  • Cross-cultural and bicultural experiences

Collaboration with therapists

I enjoy collaborating closely with therapists as part of a coordinated care team. I am happy to coordinate with your existing therapist or help you find a therapist who is a good fit.
