Welcome

I entered psychiatry out of a deep interest in understanding people and relationships, and in how meaning, connection, and healing emerge after trauma and profound suffering. My clinical training, alongside my own mental health journey, has shaped both my professional approach and the way I live my life. In my work, I aim to support others in cultivating greater connection, clarity, and fulfillment.

I take a collaborative, individualized approach to care. I view my role as helping patients access and strengthen the inner resources they already possess, meeting each person where they are. Psychotherapy is central to my work, with medications used thoughtfully and only when helpful or indicated.

Specialized care for complex, whole-person needs

I provide a systems-oriented perspective that considers individuals within their relational, cultural, and societal contexts. My practice blends evidence-based psychiatry with depth-oriented psychotherapy and mindfulness-based approaches.

Psychotherapy

Psychodynamic, relational, and mindfulness-informed therapy that supports insight, resilience, and lasting change.

Medication management

Thoughtful, conservative prescribing with a focus on shared decision-making and long-term wellbeing.

Psychedelic integration

Collaboration with therapists and integration-focused support for ketamine-assisted psychotherapy and other transformative experiences.

Who I work with

I am particularly interested in working with individuals navigating cross-cultural identities, students and professionals, and those exploring questions of identity and meaning. Many of my patients seek support for:

  • Anxiety disorders, OCD, and related conditions
  • ADHD across the lifespan
  • Trauma, attachment, and relational challenges
  • Life transitions, grief, and major decisions
  • Burnout, perfectionism, and high achievement stress

Shared humanity is central

I am drawn to treatment modalities that emphasize shared humanity. I value offering tools that have been meaningful in my own path of growth and healing, including mindfulness-based approaches and psychedelic medicine.
